# ACTION Schema

## Required Sections

- `# ACTION NAME`
- `## Purpose`
- `## When to Use`
- `## Inputs`
- `## Constraints`
- `## Process`
- `## Output`
- `## Failure Modes`
- `## Example`

## Rules

- Keep actions narrow and reusable.
- Produce structured results.
- Do not overlap heavily with another action.
